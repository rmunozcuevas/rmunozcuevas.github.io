Project Overview

This document outlines the plan and process for delivering the web solution for Jonathan Jagdon. The goal is to provide a clear roadmap and expectations.

Project Goals

  • help expand Jonathan's potential clientel for interesting in joining a Book Club
  • helping to inform users of when Book Club Meetings Occur, fill in Members on past club meeting information.

Content of Website

  • A user can submit a form for this to happen with information on what the genre of the book looks like
